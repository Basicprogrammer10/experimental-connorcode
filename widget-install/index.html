Welcome to my quickly thrown together widget install guide!

To start, go to widget.connotcode.com. Then click "Sign Up" or "Log In". Also now might be a good time to mention this only works on iOS. (Scroll down to continue)

Put in your school email (name@bernardsboe.com), click next and put in your genesis password. This is your 6 digit student ID number or an actual password if you set one.

The widget logs you in to genesis with your credentials to get your schedule. So if you ever change your password, you may need to log in again to update it. Don't worry about me seeing your password or anything, I could see that without you using the widget if I wanted to (but i dont).

If all goes well, you should now be on your account page. You will now need to download the Scriptable app. This app allows you to write code that runs on your phone and Its what I originally used to make this widget a few years ago on my first day of high school.

Now click to download the widget script. Just so you know, the script is customized for you with your account ID. This means that if anyone else gets it, they will be able to see your schedule. Which could be a good thing i guess.

Now that its downloaded, open the downloads page in Safari. (Or whatever web browser you use).

Click on the script.

Ignore all the scarry text and click the share button.

Now click the scriptable app. (You might have to scroll down to find the app).

Now that its opened in the scriptable app, click "Add to my scripts".

You can now click the little play button in the bottom right corner to make sure everything is working.

If it is, you should see your schedule. (Because we go to all of our classes on the first day the text is kinda overflowing outside of the widget, but this wont be a problem for most of the year.)

Now you can add the widget to your widget panel thing. (I like this because you can access it without unlocking your phone). Anyway, click "Edit" then the "+" button.

Select the Scriptable widget.

Choose how big you want it. I would recommend the middle size.

Add the widget and tap on it to open its configuration.

Click "Choose" to select the script to run for the widget.

Select the "Classes" script and close the menu.

And thats all! If it ever stops working for some time (like a few days) feel free to reach out, genesis may have changed how they display the schedule, which should be pretty easy to fix and you wont even have to update the script or anything.

You can configure the background gradient and text color if you would really like. I wont go into depth here but you have to edit the script in Scriptable and change the values under "// User config".
